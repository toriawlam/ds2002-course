## To create an AWS Academy account

1. Student receives an invitation email and click on “Get Started”.
![Image 1](https://media.licdn.com/dms/image/v2/C5612AQGkNZw-Zc9lmA/article-inline_image-shrink_1000_1488/article-inline_image-shrink_1000_1488/0/1598862218064?e=1775088000&v=beta&t=VKvWP24tyPpUA6Qg90y0bCvOefgv0rf3tBF0NWXdXc4)
3. For the first time, click on “Create My Account”.
![Image 2](https://media.licdn.com/dms/image/v2/C5612AQF42UfF7yS8Mw/article-inline_image-shrink_1000_1488/article-inline_image-shrink_1000_1488/0/1598862236355?e=1775088000&v=beta&t=BWrJJEaB1LW8h-OaEUc1KaslOxIJLXJcfkUTUCPXA1A)
(Don't click Canvas account as it is not your UVA Canvas account.)
5. Input a password and click on “Register”.
