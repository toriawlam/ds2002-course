# Personal reflection - final project

This is an **individual** assignment. Write in **first person** about **your own** experience. It should **complement** the group work (Milestone 1 design and Milestone 2 code and documentation). Reflect on what *you* learned and how *you* worked.

---

## Format and submission

Format your document as a **single-page PDF**. Use a readable font (about 11-12 pt) and normal margins. If your draft is longer than one page, edit until it fits on **one** page.

Submit the PDF in **Canvas** as the assignment instructs.

---

## Document organization

At the top of your document add the following information:

**Name:**  
**Computing ID:**  
**Team name:**  
**GitHub URL:**

Then add sections 1–3 with headers as described below.

### 1. What I learned

List **two or three** concrete things **you** understand better because of this project (a tool, a service, coding, debugging, documentation habits, etc.). Be specific.

### 2. A challenge, and my proudest moment

Describe an aspect of the project that you personally found challenging. What did **you** try, and what helped **you** move forward? 

Describe a contribution to the project that you're most proud of and why.

### 3. What I would do differently next time

If you did a similar project again, what would **you** change about **your** planning, individual contribution, or approach to teamwork? Write about **your** choices, not a critique of teammates.
